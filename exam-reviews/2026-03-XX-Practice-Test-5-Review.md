## 🎯 Final Exam Strategy

### Before Exam Day
- [ ] Review exam format and rules
- [ ] Know your testing center location/online setup
- [ ] Prepare ID and confirmation
- [ ] Get 7-8 hours sleep
- [ ] Eat a good breakfast

### During Exam
- **Time Management:** 130 minutes / 65 questions = 2 min/question
  - Check time at question 30 (should have 65+ min left)
  - Check time at question 50 (should have 30+ min left)
  - Save 15-20 min for review

- **Question Strategy:**
  1. Read question completely
  2. Identify key requirements
  3. Eliminate 2 wrong answers
  4. Choose best from remaining 2
  5. Flag if uncertain
  6. Move on

- **Keywords to Watch:**
  - "MOST cost-effective" = cheapest option
  - "LEAST operational overhead" = managed service
  - "HIGH performance" = low latency
  - "SECURE" = encryption, private, IAM
